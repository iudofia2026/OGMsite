ABOUT





This is Art Stdyo. A multi-disciplinary graphic design studio where ideas take shape with mind, heart, and flairbringing value to your brand, just as our name suggests. Art means plus in Turkish, reflecting our approachnot just designing, but elevating brands with meaning, clarity, and purpose.

At the heart of our work lies curiosity. We ask questionsto the people and brands we collaborate with, and, of course, to ourselves. By defining needs with precision, we gain a deeper understanding of each project we take on, allowing us to craft more meaningful solutions.

Every idea deserves precision, driven by mind. In each project, we seek a unique story that shines with intelligence. We believe aesthetics should be driven by meaning rather than mere superficiality.

Our process unfolds in carefully structured phases, progressing step by step. We are committed to delivering thoughtful, refined, and meticulously curated ideas to our partners.

At the core of everything we do, a witty idea emerges with flaircrafted with attention to detail, infused with aesthetic sensibility, and rooted in a story that makes it whole.

If youre looking for a visual story like this, lets meet.





Aksel Ceylan
Co-founder + Creative Director

Aksel Ceylan is a multidisciplinary designer and creative director based in New York City.


He studied graphic design at Accademia Italiana in Florence and Marmara University in Istanbul.

He started his career in 2009 at independent design studios before moving on to leading network agencies like J. Walter Thompson and Publicis, where he expanded his expertise in advertising, communication, and art direction. In 2018, after nearly a decade of industry experience, he moved to the United States upon receiving an offer to collaborate with renowned designer Peter Arnell, marking a transition to a broader global practice. Following this collaboration, he served as a creative director for SHVO, leading major rebranding efforts for their expanding luxury real estate and hospitality portfolio a partnership that continues today. He later broadened his experience at Lipman Studio, founded by legendary creative director David Lipman, before co-founding +Stdyo.

Aksels work has been recognized for creative excellence and strategic impact. He leads +Stdyo, dedicated to building story-driven, culturally resonant brand identities and visual narratives.
Kaan Duygu
Co-founder + Creative Director
Kaan Duygu is an Istanbul-based art director, graphic designer, and illustrator.

His passion for graphic design emerged during high school, eventually leading him to earn a degree in Graphic Design from Marmara University. Kaan began his career at Alametifarika, a leading Istanbul agency, where he quickly demonstrated his creative potential. He later worked with renowned global network agencies such as TeamRed (Vodafones exclusive creative agency) and VMLY&R under the WPP group, contributing to successful campaigns for prominent international brands.

Kaans creative talent has been widely acknowledged, including being recognized as one of the top emerging creatives in his field with a 30 Under 30 honor. His work has also earned international acclaim, such as Cannes Lions, showcasing his ability to deliver innovative and impactful designs.
Ard Duygu Ceylan
Creative Project Manager
Ard Duygu Ceylan is a multi-talented professional based in New York City, with expertise in project management, photography, videography, and music.

She holds dual degrees in Radio, Television, Cinema, and Advertising from Kadir Has University in Istanbul, Trkiye. Her career began at Dream TV, where she gained experience in editing, directing, and producing. She also appeared on camera as a presenter, interviewing celebrities and engaging audiences.

Ard has worked in creative hubs like New York, Los Angeles, and Miami, collaborating on projects with brands such as Mandarin Oriental, Transamerica Pyramid, and The Raleigh. Her expertise in architectural, documentary, and portrait photography reflects her passion for visual storytelling.

She also has a background in music, with her projects on Spotify and YouTube garnering hundreds of thousands of views and listens.
Kevser Aka
Graphic Designer + Illustrator
Kevser Aka is a graphic designer and illustrator based in Bursa, Trkiye. She graduated from Dokuz Eyll University, Department of Graphic Design.

With a background that spans painting and graphic arts, Kevsers work reflects her interest in branding, editorial design, and typography. In 2023, she was recognized as the Young Graphic Designer of the Year by the Turkish Graphic Designers Association.
Bora Civelek
Graphic + Motion Designer
Bora Civelek is a graphic and motion designer based in Ankara, Trkiye. He graduated from Bilkent University, Department of Graphic Design, in 2022.

He began working on his first projects during high school and continued to take on freelance work throughout university, gaining experience in various areas of design. While he is interested in nearly all aspects of graphic design, his primary focus is on branding, motion design, and 3D design.

Eda Kanmaz
Graphic Designer
Eda Kanmaz is a graphic designer based in Istanbul, Trkiye. She earned her degree in Graphic Design from Mimar Sinan Fine Arts University, Faculty of Fine Arts, in 2025.

Her journey in graphic design began during high school, and she further honed her skills through freelance projects throughout university. Passionate about visual communication, she focuses on expanding her expertise in user interface design and typography.
Gkay Abanoz
Senior Software Engineer

Gkay Abanoz is a full-stack developer based in New York City, bringing over seven years of experience in designing and building digital systems that strike a balance between performance, usability, and scalability.


He has contributed to high-traffic platforms in travel, e-commerce, and wellness, leading the development of custom dashboards, internal tools, and end-to-end digital solutions.


At Art Stdyo, Gkay combines technical precision with creative collaborationbridging design and development to bring ideas to life through thoughtful code and smart systems.
